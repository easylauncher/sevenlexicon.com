ROHIT TANEJA

Rohit Taneja LL.B. graduated from the Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University. He is a Civil and Corporate lawyer based in New Delhi and has enrolled with the Bar Council of Delhi as well as the Delhi High Court Bar Association.Rohit Taneja is an experienced practitioner handling a wide variety of Civil, Criminal as well as Corporate Commercial disputes across various judicial forums throughout India.

Rohit has advised many foreign clients with regards to investment in the Indian market and has also helped them to set up their base in India and other operational matters.Furthermore, Rohit also specializes in acquisitions and takeovers by both Indian as well as foreign companies. He has also advised clients with regards to restructuring of their existing business and joint ventures.
